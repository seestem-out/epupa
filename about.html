About us

Mission and Vision

Epupa Films is dedicated to amplifying African voices through cinema.

Who are we?

Epupa Films is an emerging film studio that focuses on creating compelling narratives rooted in African culture and storytelling. Established with the vision of showcasing the rich heritage and diverse stories of the African continent, Epupa Films aims to produce high-quality films that resonate with both local and international audiences.

Promote Cultural Heritage

By telling stories that reflect the traditions, struggles, and triumphs of various African communities.
